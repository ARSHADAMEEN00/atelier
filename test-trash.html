Juvederm – Injectable Lip Fillers In Dubai

The Cause

The skin around your lips loses the moisture, fullness, and volume as we age. The skin on our lips is constantly moisturized from within by hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring sugar. However, the skin loses hyaluronic acid over time, and lines begin to appear around the mouth.

The Effect

The loss of hyaluronic acid leads to the appearance of wrinkles and the loss of fullness of our lips. As our lips become thinner, they lose volume and lack definition. We can develop vertical, or “marionette” lines around the mouth, which can form at the corners going down towards the chin or we can even develop a droop to the corners of the mouth, giving our lips a sad appearance.

The Treatment

The common signs of ageing can be reversed to a great degree with Juvederm fillers. Lip fillers have been widely accepted by celebrities across the world and are being adopted by both men and women in Dubai to give shape, structure, and volume our lips need. Lip fillers treatment is done by injecting Hyaluronic Acid (HA) based filler into the lip to increase the volume and size. The gel present in the filler supports and shapes the tissue of the lips. Lucia Aesthetic Clinic uses the most popular lip fillers procedure by Juvederm which is great for targeting fine lines around the area and giving the desired volume to the lips. One can get this done on both lips, just the top or just the bottom one, to increase volume or contour them. Since lip filler procedure involves a delicate area like lips, it is always recommended to consult our highly experienced doctor.

The Procedure

A detailed consultation on lip fillers with Dr Radmila Lukian at Lucia Aesthetic Clinic Dubai will guide you to understand the exact area that will be injected to best complement with the rest of your facial features. It is advised not to exercise, massage or have facial on the day of your procedure. On the procedure day, expectations are set by carefully marking the areas to be injected. The procedure itself takes around 15 to 20 minutes. Depending on the sensitivity of the patient, we apply a numbing cream prior to the treatment to make the procedure pain free. Although the procedure is pain-free, one might feel a pinch at the beginning as the filler is injected. Generally, either ice is applied on the area or massage is given for 15 minutes to ensure no bumps are formed before you are good to go.

After The Procedure

As soon as the lip filler procedure is over, the results will be immediately visible. The numbness, however, may take about 15 minutes to settle down. Lip injections may cause a bit of discomfort during the first 24 to 48 hours with associated swelling and tenderness in the lips. Hydration is crucial for getting a good result so it is advised to drink plenty of water after lip injection and the patient should hold off strenuous exercises and very heated environments including steam rooms. The effect of lip fillers can last from 6 to 9 months. The effect time of the procedure varies on the type of filler and the age of the patient.
