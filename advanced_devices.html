HIFU

Do you want to look younger but not go through the pain? HIFU is a remarkable breakthrough in technology, offering a non-invasive alternative to surgical lifting. HIFU is used on the face to help improve the tone and all the features of your face such as your eyes, cheeks, around the mouth, and chin, making it a viable alternative to cosmetic injections.

Ultrasonic cavitation

Ultrasonic Cavitation is a new technology that is using narrow frequency waves to break down unwanted fat cells. Fat cells get dissolved and absorbed to the blood stream and then excreted in your urine.Treatments starts with selected body areas and is conducted in circular movement of applicator in the selected body area.
