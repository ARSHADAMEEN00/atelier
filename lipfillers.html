Natural Looking Lip Fillers in Dubai

Lip fillers are a very popular non-surgical method of lip augmentation that uses hyaluronic acid-based dermal fillers such add volume, shape, and structure to smaller, thin lips as well as help smooth away wrinkles and lip lines. With our lip enhancement procedure at our Umm Suqeim Clinic in Dubai, you can get the perfect lip aesthetic after just one appointment. Our highly experienced certified aesthetics doctors have been performing lip augmentation safely and successfully to produce natural results for years. As is the case with most cosmetic procedures, lip injections especially require a high degree of precision.

Full, luscious lips indicate youthfulness and sensuality, and are coveted by celebrities and ordinary women alike. Unfortunately, like many other areas of the face, the lips begin to lose their volume as we age, growing thin and lusterless. A once beautiful smile can be marred by lips that have lost their definition, causing a patient to feel self-conscious and frustrated with their appearance.

Aestetic Issues Addressed By Lip Fillers

  • Enhancement and plumping of the overall size of both lips

  • Correction of the balance of lower to upper lip

  • Enhancement of the points of the bow of the medial upper lip, which can descend due to lost volume through aging or which may not be prominent in those with thin lips

  • Enhancement of the upper lip structures located between the nose and mouth

FAQ about Lip Fillers

Is lip filler right for me ?

We recommend Lip Filler injections for patients seeking safe and immediate smoothing of the skin around the mouth area. These small changes can have a dramatic impact on the youthful appearance of your entire face.

How soon will l see results of lip fillers ?

You will notice changes in your appearance right away—that’s the beauty of non-surgical facial treatments. Lip filler injections will instantly restore your mouths natural beauty and plumpness .

How long do lip fillers last ?

Most dermal lip fillers will last in the range of four to six months before requiring another session. This makes lip fillers a great option if you are unsure about the size, shape or volume you are looking to achieve or if you are undergoing lip augmentation for the first time.

How long does the lip filler procedure take ?

10 – 30 minutes per treatment
