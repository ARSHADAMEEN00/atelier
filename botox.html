Botulinum toxin

In Dubai one of the most common and most effective treatment for men and women who want to do something about their wrinkles but are unable to take time away from work for more invasive treatments is anti wrinkle injections. Unlike some cosmetic medical treatments, anti wrinkle injections are suitable for virtually anyone who has wrinkles or other skin lines that they want to remove. This includes people who want to fight the signs of natural ageing, or those who’ve developed lines from exposure to the sun.

How do Anti Wrinkle Injectables Work

Anti-wrinkle injections can achieve a tighter, smoother appearance. Anti-wrinkle injections block the chemical signal that the brain sends to the muscle telling the muscle to contract. In effect the treatment relaxes and lifts the muscle, softening the look of lines and giving a tighter, smoother appearance. It generally takes three to 10 days after the treatment for the maximum to develop fully and re-treatment should not occur within that period. The effect can last up to four months, although with regular treatments it may be possible to reduce the dosage.

FAQ about Botulinum toxin

Am l a good candidate for Anti Wrinkle Treatment ?

Anti Wrinkle treatments are like a liquid facelift—safe, refreshing, and rejuvenating for your whole face. Anti Wrinkle injections are best suited for eliminating moderate to severe frown lines, crow’s feet, laugh lines, and other fine wrinkles of the face. Ideal candidates for Anti Wrinkle injections are adults who may be unhappy about the appearance of dynamic wrinkles of the upper face. Eligible patients are healthy with no history of neuromuscular disease and have good skin quality.
