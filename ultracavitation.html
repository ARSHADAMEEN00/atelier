The Ultrasound Cavitation Treatment Process

Lipo cavitation also targets fat deposits, but the procedure is non-invasive and has virtually no side effects or recovery time.
Lipo cavitation uses ultrasound — sound waves — to break up fat cells stubbornly clinging below the skin. The fat cell membrane is disintegrated, and the body’s natural detoxifying processes take care of the rest. The ultrasound frequency used only affects fat cells, so no other tissues are damaged during treatment.

How Many Cavitation Treatments Are Needed?

Clients who chose to have ultrasonic cavitation are able to see visible reductions in fat in the first week, and a treatment schedule of 10 to 20 weekly sessions will typically help you achieve the desired weight and inch loss.
Before your first treatment, you’ll meet with Dr. Azmal for a physical exam and consultation.
In most cases, the ideal candidate for Ultrasonic Cavitation is someone moderately active and a healthy eater. Ultrasonic Cavitation is not intended to treat morbid obesity but works most effectively to remove unwanted fat from targeted areas of the body.

Ultrasound Cavitation Sessions

One Ultrasonic Cavitation session often results in a 1-5cm reduction in the layer of fat. However, for optimum results, you may require multiple sessions , depending on the layer of fat being treated. Each session is conducted no longer than 2-3 days apart; this is to ensure the cells do not reabsorb the fat. This also allows us to monitor your progression and cater to your every need at each step of the way to ensure optimal results*. .
