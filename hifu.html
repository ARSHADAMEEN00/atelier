HIFU Treatment in Dubai

Do you want to look younger but not go through the pain? HIFU is a remarkable breakthrough in technology, offering a non-invasive alternative to surgical lifting. HIFU is used on the face to help improve the tone and all the features of your face such as your eyes, cheeks, around the mouth, and chin, making it a viable alternative to cosmetic injections.

What is HIFU?

HIFU uses ultrasound energy to heat cells far beneath the surface of the skin, stimulating the production of more collagen. This increase in collagen, which is the protein responsible for skin elasticity and firmness, results in tighter-looking skin with fewer wrinkles and improved smoothness. HIFU is quick, non-invasive and relatively painless. Since there is no damage done to the upper layers of skin, there is no downtime involved in this treatment, and customers can go about their day as normal post-treatment.

FAQ about HIFU Treatment

What can be treated using HIFU ?

Brow lifting.
Jowl lifting.
Mid face lifting.
Nasolabial fold reduction.
Neck lifting and tightening.
Overall skin tightening and rejuvenation.

Who is recommended for this treatment ?

We recommend this for people who want to tighten up their facial areas and get rid of fine lines by plumping up the tissue naturally. It’s safe to use after anti wrinkle injection as well but we would suggest doing the HIFU treatment at least one week after.

Is HIFU Treatment safe ?

Absolutely. The procedure is non-invasive – meaning the procedure does not penetrate the skin so there is no risk of infection or complication. The procedure itself acts as a catalyst to to stimulate the body’s natural processes leaving with you natural results.

Can HIFU be combined with other treatments ?

Often HIFU is used alongside other treatments to enhance results. For a lifting effect it is often used with Threadlift to give an optimum lift.
