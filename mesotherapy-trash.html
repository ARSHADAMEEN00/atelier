Mesotherapy in Dubai

Mesotherapy

Skin Therapy or Deep Hydration (also known as Revolumization Process) is a procedure in which the skin of the face, neck and chest area is covered with a numbing lotion, after which thousands of painless injections administer Nutrition, Vitamins and Hyaluronic Acid to the dermis. Hyaluronic Acid (HA) is the most basic building block in our body, but unfortunately as we age, the amount of the HA in the skin diminishes. In Mesotherapy sessions our patients have the skin Hydrated and Revolumized, as they all feel the skin get tighter and younger after the treatment. Our patients say the face feels fuller and skin tighter like it used to be when they were younger.

  • Mesotherapy for Anti Aging

    Mesotherapy is a treatment of choice to achieve radiant, luminous and firm skin. Aging is manifested by various signs that change the physiognomy: A change of the quality of the skin: appearance of fine lines, dilated pores, slackening, loss of radiance and tonicity A change in form: appearance of wrinkles, loss and displacement of volume. Negative facial expressions: the face expresses sadness, fatigue, fear, anger, disgust, contempt, surprise The injected products are mainly a mixture of hyaluronic acid, vitamins, minerals, amino acids, etc. They stimulate fibroblast activity by giving it the natural nutrients it needs to regenerate skin cells.

  • Mesotherapy for Acne Treatment

    In patients with acne-prone skin, there is a dysfunction due to the alteration of the sebum composition. The disorder causes the appearance of acne, blackheads or whiteheads.Mesotherapy helps to reduce the production of oil in the skin, thus reducing acne. It also helps with the stimulation of new cells which reduces the dead skin cells, in turn, making your skin look brighter.

  • Mesotherapy for Removal of Acner Scars

    The scar is an indentation created by a wund that heals below the normal layer of skin tissue. Atrophic scars are indented with clear delineations. It is caused by the destruction of collagen during an inflammatory condition such as cystic acne or chickenpox. Mesotherapy helps this by stimulating new skin cells to grow and repair the area. Unlike common acne treatments, creams or serums mesotherapy allows the products to penetrate the skin further allowing them to do their job in a more effective way.

  • Mesotherapy for Hair Loss

    Hair loss is one of the most common problems today. Though losing 25 to 50 hair strands in a day is normal, sometimes, the hair loss can be a lot more and rapid, causing worry and concern. Mesotherapy may help prevent hair loss, improve hair quality, and stimulate hair growth. The mesotherapy hair product acts directly in the bulb of the capillary. The vitamins, amino acids, antioxidants, plant stem cells and hyaluronic acid slow down hair loss and promote growth and vitality.

We recommend starting with an intensive course of treatments every 2 weeks for the first 2-3 months.

Mesotherapy is minimally invasive, requiring little downtime. Slight redness and irritation are expected and usually settles very quickly within 48 hours
