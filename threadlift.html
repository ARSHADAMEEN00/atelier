What is PDO Thread lifting

Over the course of time as we grow older many changes happen to the appearance and shape of your face as a result of ageing. Ageing does not only affect the elasticity of your skin but also you suffer bone and fat loss and cartilage. The good thing is that you can age gracefull and beautifully with as there are treatments and procedures which can help you reverse and reduce the signs of aging and maintain your youthful look whilst improving the health of your skin and face. One of the most sorted after procedure is the PDO thread lifting which we provide at our clinic in Dubai A PDO Thread Lift is a non-surgical facelift with PDO threads, suitable if you have loose skin around your mid to lower face and are looking for a tighter and fresher appearance. The treatment is a safe and effective alternative to surgical procedures.

Who is a good Candidate for a thread lift ?

You may be a suitable candidate for thread lift procedures if you’re bothered by early on signs of aging but aren’t ready to commit to invasive surgical procedures. Thread lifts can be a good fit for men and women that want to subtly tighten and tone without scarring or extended recovery time.

Are the results of threadlifts permanent ?

Thread lifts are not permanent, but they do last for a long time. The biodegradable PDO polymer is absorbed by the body over a period of 12-24 months. You may need to come back for another PDO thread lift touch up in 12 months.

Is thread lifting procedure painful ?

You might feel a minor discomfort during the procedure, but we will apply a local anesthetic to the area being treated to help with any pain.

Since this is a minimally invasive procedure, you will be able to continue with your regular activities as soon as the next day.
